99 Nights in the Forest WIKI

99 Nights in the Forest is a survival horror experience set deep in a mysterious woodland. Players must endure nightly threats, gather resources, and uncover the secrets hidden within the forest. With dynamic enemies, crafting systems, and evolving lore, each night brings new challenges. Whether solo or with friends, survival depends on strategy and courage.

🔶 Core Survival Mechanics

Players must survive 99 nights in a terrifying forest while searching for four missing children. The game uses a dynamic difficulty system where monster attack frequency and strength increase as nights progress.

  • 🌞 Daytime Activities: Collect resources, build defenses, explore the map
  • 🌚 Nighttime Survival: Protect the campfire, avoid monsters, maintain light sources

📜 Inspiration from Real Events

The game is inspired by a real-life 2023 plane crash in Colombia where four children survived 40 days in the Amazon rainforest. Developers combined this event with supernatural horror elements.

☠️ Primary Threat: Wendigo

  • 👹 A supernatural creature from folklore – deer head, human body
  • 💡 Light-averse – can be driven away with campfires and torches
  • 🔊 Active at night – makes unique sounds when approaching
  • 🌲 Forest hostility increases with each child rescued
