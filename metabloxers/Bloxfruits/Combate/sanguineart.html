Sanguine Art Blox Fruits

Sanguine Art is an elegant and deadly fighting style in Blox Fruits, introduced in Update 20. It blends martial power and supernatural elements for a unique, strategic playstyle.

Type Fighting Style
Update Update 20
Cost $5,000,000 + 5,000 Fragments
Requeriments Leviathan Heart + Materials

How to Unlock Sanguine Art

  • 1. Defeat the Leviathan using a harpoon from the Beast Hunter ship to obtain the Leviathan Heart.
  • 2. Talk to Shafi near the Island Boy NPCs at the Tiki Outpost.
  • 3. Give Materials: 20 Demonic Wisps, 20 Vampire Fangs, 2 Dark Fragments + Leviathan Heart.
  • 4. Pay $5,000,000 and 5,000 Fragments to learn the style.

Godhuman vs Sanguine Art

  • Choose Godhuman for brute strength; Sanguine for tactical, high-skill fights.
  • Sanguine Art: Offers area control, tracking projectiles, and elegant, precise attacks.

Training & Mastery

  • Sanguine Art demands timing and practice. Unlike brawler styles, it rewards planning, combo flow, and enemy prediction.
  • Invest time mastering its range, delays, and zoning tactics to fully unleash its potential in PvP or raids.
