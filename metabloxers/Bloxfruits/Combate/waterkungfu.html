Water Kung Fu Blox Fruits

Water Kung Fu is a dynamic and powerful fighting style in Blox Fruits known for its quick attacks and useful area-of-effect (AoE) abilities. It is ideal for farming in the early to mid-game and serves as a stepping stone to the stronger Sharkman Karate.

Type Fighting Style
Price $750,000
Location Underwater City (First Sea)
Upgradable Yes (Sharkman Karate)

How to Unlock Sanguine Art

  • Purchase Water Kung Fu from the Water Kung Fu Teacher for $750,000.
  • You can find the teacher in:
  • Underwater City (First Sea)
  • Hot side of Hot & Cold (Second Sea)
  • Castle on the Sea (Third Sea)
