Game Overview & Mechanics

Core Gameplay

Dive into the chaotic world of Steal a Brainrot! Build your base, collect bizarre Brainrot creatures, and rack up huge amounts of in-game Cash. Your income depends on the rarity and special Mutations of your Characters.

The ultimate objective is to become the richest player on the server, either by strategically collecting Brainrots or by boldly raiding your rivals' bases to steal theirs! The game is designed for seamless action across PC, mobile, and tablet.

Key Gameplay Elements

  • Get Brainrots: Grab new Brainrots from the central conveyor belt using Cash
  • Raid and Steal: Sneak into other players' bases to steal their Brainrots
  • Make Money: Brainrots automatically generate cash every second
  • Secure Your Base: Use "Lockdown" feature for 60-second laser wall protection
  • Rebirth for Power: Sacrifice progress for permanent cash multipliers
  • Use Chaotic Tools: Buy Items like Blackhole Slap, Laser Gun, Ban Hammer

Controls

  • PC Movement: WASD or arrow keys
  • Mobile Movement: On-screen joystick
  • Interact/Steal: E key (PC) or tap (mobile)
  • Platforms: PC, Mobile, Tablet optimized
